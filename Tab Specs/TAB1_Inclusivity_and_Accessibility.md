\# Tab 1 — Inclusivity \& Accessibility (Built Environment)



\## Entrances \& Getting In

\- Step-free entrance

\- Accessible entrance clearly signed

\- Automatic / power-assisted door

\- Door width fits mobility devices

\- Threshold / lip minimized

\- Entrance lighting adequate

\- Weather protection at entrance (awning/vestibule)



\## Ramps \& Slopes

\- Ramp available where needed

\- Ramp slope manageable

\- Ramp has handrails

\- Ramp landing space adequate



\## Elevators \& Stairs (Vertical Movement)

\- Elevator / lift available (if multi-level)

\- Elevator reachable from accessible route

\- Elevator doors open wide enough

\- Elevator controls reachable

\- Elevator has braille / tactile buttons

\- Elevator has visual floor indicator

\- Elevator has audible cues / announcements

\- Stairs have handrails

\- Stair edges / nosing high-contrast



\## Routes \& Hallways (Inside Movement)

\- Pathways clear of obstacles

\- Hallways wide enough for two-way mobility devices

\- Doorways along route are accessible

\- Seating/rest point available along long routes



\## Wayfinding \& Signage

\- High-contrast signage

\- Braille / tactile signage

\- Directional signage to accessible routes

\- Accessible map / directory available

\- Consistent naming (room/building names match signs)

\- Information desk / help point clearly marked (if present)



\## Hearing \& Vision Supports (In Space)

\- Hearing loop / assistive listening available (if applicable)

\- Assistive listening clearly signed (if applicable)

\- Visual alarm / strobe present (if applicable)

\- Audible alarm present (if applicable)

\- Captions available on public screens (if applicable)



\## Washrooms — Types \& Access

\- Women’s washroom present

\- Men’s washroom present

\- All-gender washroom present

\- Single-user washroom present

\- Accessible washroom stall present

\- Accessible sink clearance

\- Grab bars present

\- Mirror usable from seated position

\- Wheelchair accessible shower present (if applicable)

\- Infant change table present (if applicable)

\- Menstrual product dispenser present (if applicable)



\## Washrooms — Gender Diversity \& Signage

\- All-gender washroom signage is clear and respectful

\- Single-user washroom uses gender-neutral signage

\- Washroom signage includes accessibility information (where relevant)

\- Washroom signage is easy to find from main hallway

\- If signage is missing/unclear, “Absent” is recorded (this is the audit signal)



\## Parenting, Care \& Personal Needs

\- Lactation / breastfeeding / parenting room present

\- Adult change table present (if applicable)

\- Water fountain(s) accessible

\- Bottle filling station accessible (if present)



\## Sensory \& Comfort

\- Quiet / sensory-friendly space available (if applicable)

\- Low-stimulation waiting area available (if applicable)

\- Scent-free area identified (if applicable)



\## Exterior \& Mobility (Around the Building)

\- Accessible parking nearby

\- Accessible drop-off zone nearby

\- Curb cuts present and aligned

\- Exterior pathways smooth and wide

\- Exterior lighting adequate

\- Service animal relief area nearby



\## Safety \& Emergency

\- Emergency intercom / call button present (if applicable)

\- AED present (if applicable)

\- Naloxone kit present (if applicable)

\- Fire extinguisher cabinet reachable / accessible

\- Emergency exits accessible

\- Emergency wayfinding / guiding signage present
